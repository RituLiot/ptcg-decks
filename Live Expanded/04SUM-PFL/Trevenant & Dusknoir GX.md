![decklist](../../!Images/Live%20Expanded/4SUM-PFL/Trevenant%20%26%20Dusknoir%20GX.png)

## Thoughts
With each passing turn that you first use _Night Watch_ your chances of winning go drastically down. Get it turn 1 - and you're golden. However, turbo nature of this deck strips it off versatility, and there are ways to play around it. Heck, _Path to the Peak_ on turn 1, and you're scramblnig to do anything. Glass cannon of a deck.
